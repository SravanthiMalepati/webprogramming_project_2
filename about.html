Info for Game Play


  • Jeopardy is a two player game. Player1 gets the opening chance.

  • Player can choose any question from one of the five categories. Each category has questions ranging from $10 to $50.

  • If a player gets the question right, he/she will be awarded the score associated with that particular question.

  • If selected answer is wrong, then the control goes to other player.

  • Player with most number of points at the end of the game wins.
